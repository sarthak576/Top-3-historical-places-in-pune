Top 3 Historical Places in Pune...

Shaniwar Wada

It is a historic fortification located in Pune, India, built in the 18th century by the Peshwas. It is known for its grand architecture and historical significance.

Aga Khan Palace

Aga Khan Palace in Pune, India, built in 1892, is known for its historical significance as a prison for Mahatma Gandhi during the Quit India Movement.

Sinhagad Fort

It is a historic hill fortress known for its strategic importance in Maratha history. It played a key role in many battles and offers a popular trekking destination.
